About Mark

6/Mar 2015 1 min. read

Mark first touched the Internet in 1991 when he was sponsored to do undergraduate research. After graduation and Internet withdrawl, he joined the first national commercial Internet Service Provider (before the world wide web). One of the web masters of Netscape, cnnfn.com, and sgi.com, Mark was part of the world’s largest and earliest media and web sites. Mark progressed to Developer Relations, where he became the JavaScript and LDAP Technology Evangelist.
